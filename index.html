Step 1: Establishing the Ownership Bridge

Step 1: Agreement → Property owner contracts with Token-Lebnani to tokenize the real estate.
Step 2: ADGM Holding Company → We create a Holding Company in Abu Dhabi. It will issue tokens and be owned 100% by token holders.
Step 3: Lebanese SPV → The ADGM Holding Company incorporates a Lebanese SPV, which will legally hold the property title, satisfying local law.

Key Insight: We build the legal vessels first. TLB provides the infrastructure; we do not invest in or own the property.

Step 2: Onboarding the Asset & Manager

Step 4: Title Transfer → The owner transfers the property to the Lebanese SPV. The 6% Lebanese registration tax is paid once, here.
Step 5: Appoint Facility Manager (FMC) → The SPV contracts an independent FMC to handle all operations: collect rent (in USD), pay expenses, and send net income to the UAE.

Key Insight: The FMC is a service provider under strict contract. They manage the hassle; you receive the net income.

Step 3: How You Become an Owner

Step 6: Token Issuance → Tokens representing 100% of the asset are minted to the owner, who can sell any portion on our platform.
Step 7: Investor Onboarding → You complete ADGM-compliant KYC, sign smart contract and legal agreements. Atomic settlement delivers tokens to your wallet.
Step 8: Your Rights & Exit → You receive automated USD payouts, voting rights, and full ADGM legal recourse. Resell tokens anytime on the secondary market.

Key Insight: Your ownership is direct, legally enforceable, and designed for liquidity. The one-time 6% tax is behind you.
